I am a PhD student in the [Cvijovic Lab](https://www.cvijoviclab.org/) at the Mathematics Department of the University of Gothenburg. My research focuses on developing software and models to study dynamic biological systems.

To facilitate efficient workflows when working with dynamic models, I have developed two Julia packages: [SBMLImporter.jl](https://github.com/sebapersson/SBMLImporter.jl) and [PEtab.jl](https://github.com/sebapersson/PEtab.jl). In my modeling work, I have used single-cell dynamic modeling to study nutrient signaling and reaction-diffusion models to investigate Cdc42 polarization in budding yeast.
